The quick terms 💛

YOUR SPANISH ROADMAP · AVENTUREROS CLUB

Nothing scary here — just so we're on the same page before you share your voice. ¡Vámonos!

What this is

A little tool to record yourself speaking Spanish and get a link you can drop in the Aventureros Club so others can cheer you on. That's it.

Where your clip goes

When you tap Get my link, your recording is saved online and you get a link to it. Anyone with that link can listen — there's no password on it. So please don't record anything private or personal. Keep it to your Spanish practice. 🎤

Your voice = your call

Recording and sharing is completely your choice. If you don't tap the button, nothing is ever saved or uploaded — re-record as many times as you like first.

Changed your mind?

No problem at all. Email lucia@yourspanishroadmap.com and I'll delete your clip. Clips may also be cleared out automatically after a while to keep things tidy.

Please be kind

Only share recordings that are yours, keep it friendly, and don't upload anything you wouldn't want the community to hear. 💛

The honest small print

This is a free practice tool offered as-is, with love but no guarantees — links and clips could occasionally break or disappear. By sharing, you're okay with the above. If anything ever feels off, just message me.
